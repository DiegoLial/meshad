how it works

02

Any company self-serves a campaign into a second-price auction — no minimums, no salesperson.

privacy is the product

The client can only emit five signals — the schema is public, versioned, and enforced on both ends. A batch with any extra field is rejected in full.

0 prompts, ever

Only timing metadata leaves the machine: when the agent starts/stops thinking, a rotatable anonymous ID, and the terminal type.

Terminal-native

One clearly-labeled sponsored line, block, or bordered panel — colors and emoji, never raw escape codes from an advertiser.

Publisher revenue

70% to the developer by default, in a double-entry ledger, settled via Stripe Connect once you're onboarded.
